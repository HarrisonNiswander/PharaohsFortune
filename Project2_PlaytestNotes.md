# Play Test Notes

## Playtester 1: Dylan Tate
What players understood quickly - How to move, how to collect jewels 

Where they were confused - Not confused, intructions were clear

What felt satisfying/frustrating - Winning was satisfying, some controls were not. When moving, there is a bit of a stutter which is not satisfying to play with. All of the jewels spawn at once, which makes it really easy to get 5 jewels.

## Post Playtest 1
After the comments on playtest1, we adjusted the player's movement. This got rid of the stutter. We also changed the game so only 1 jewel spawns at a time.

## Playtester 2: Josh Roper
What players understood quickly - Player movement and game mechanics

Where they were confused - Intructions were clear

What felt satisfying/frustrating - Game graphics look really, really good and the sounds fit well. When playing in multiplayer, player2 can double jump, but player1 cannot. This leads to player2 having an unfair advantage.

## Post Playtest 2 
Based on the feedback from playtester 2, we adjusted the jumping ability. We made it so player2 can no longer double jumping, making them equal with player1.
