Pharaoh’s Treasure Design Brief 

Pharaoh’s Treasure is an Egyptian themed game where the player tries to jump to platforms and collect the jewels. In the game, there are two options for playing modes: a single player and a multiplayer. In single player mode, the player races against the clock to try and collect all five jewels that randomly spawn around the map. In multiplayer, both players are racing to try and be the first to collect five jewels. In each mode, jewels are spawned in one at a time. The next jewel isn’t spawned until the previous jewel is collected. The target player experience is that the players will be engaging in a fast-paced chase to collect the jewels while utilizing the different controls (run, jump, and jump down). The core mechanic in the game is using the WASD keys to run and jump from various platforms. When there is a collision between the player’s character and a jewel, the jewel is collected. When you load into the game, you start at the menu screen and then move to the mode to select (single or multiplayer mode). After your selection, you have an instruction page that is tailored to your choice. Upon pressing space, the game will fade to the game's level, and the game begins. Upon collecting the five jewels, the game goes to the game over screen showing the results. Finally, you have the option to play again or return to the main menu. The scoring in the game depends on the game mode selected. In single player mode, the player’s score is the time it takes them to collect all five jewels. In multiplayer mode, the player’s score is based on who collects five jewels first. As mentioned above, collision is used between the character (rectangle shape) and the jewels (rectangle shape). The collision check is whether the two are overlapping. If so, then the jewel is “collected” and disappears from the screen so the next jewel can spawn. Our initial idea for the game was to have just a single player game, but as we were developing, we wanted to add something more to the game, so we added the multiplayer option.
